+ # OctoAcme Project Management: Process Overview
+ 
+ Welcome to the centralized hub for OctoAcme’s project management practices. This repository is designed to give every team member easy access to processes, decisions, and artifacts, supporting consistent and repeatable execution.
+ 
+ ---
+ 
+ ## Why Centralize Our Processes?
+ 
+ - Gather scattered PM knowledge in a searchable, version-controlled space
+ - Convert team knowledge into living documentation, accessible for onboarding and reference
+ - Support continual improvement through collaborative updates and transparent rationale
+ 
+ ---
+ 
+ ## Core Principles
+ 
+ - **Customer-first:** Prioritize customer value and usability.
+ - **Iterative delivery:** Deliver work in small, testable increments.
+ - **Clear ownership:** Every project is led by a PM and a Product Lead.
+ - **Data-informed:** Decisions are guided by evidence and measurable impact.
+ - **Psychological safety:** Open feedback and learning are encouraged.
+ 
+ ---
+ 
+ ## Key Phases of Project Management
+ 
+ 1. **Initiation**  
+    - Align on business need, outcomes, and stakeholders.
+    - Use a one-pager to clarify the problem, metrics, and team roles.
+ 
+ 2. **Planning**  
+    - Translate ideas into actionable backlogs with clear acceptance criteria.
+    - Map out timelines, roles, test plans, and dependencies.
+    - Maintain risk registers and milestone maps.
+ 
+ 3. **Execution & Tracking**  
+    - Use a visible project board (e.g., GitHub Projects: Backlog → Done) and rigorous PR process.
+    - Track velocity, demo often, and clarify blockers/escalations.
+    - Ensure robust automated and manual testing.
+ 
+ 4. **Release & Deployment**  
+    - Checklists for pre-release, safe deployment, incident handling, and stakeholder announcements.
+    - Rollback and mitigation plans established.
+ 
+ 5. **Retrospective & Continuous Improvement**  
+    - Run structured retros after each milestone.
+    - Capture actionable learnings, assign owners, and measure improvements.
+ 
+ ---
+ 
+ ## Roles & Responsibilities
+ 
+ - **Project Manager (PM):** Drives scheduling, risk, and delivery.
+ - **Product Manager (PdM):** Owns the "what" and "why", defines success.
+ - **Developers:** Build, test, review, and document.
+ - **QA/Testing:** Validate releases meet quality standards.
+ - **Stakeholders:** Provide input, feedback, and business context.
+ 
+ See `docs/octoacme-roles-and-personas.md` for detailed persona definitions.
+ 
+ ---
+ 
+ ## Risk Management & Communication
+ 
+ - Maintain a risk register (impact, probability, owner, mitigation, status)
+ - Communicate updates weekly, share status, blockers, and decisions
+ - Follow clear escalation paths for urgent risks or incidents
+ 
+ ---
+ 
+ ## How to Contribute/Improve
+ 
+ - Use the [issue template](../.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/add-update-content-to-process-docs.yml) to propose process updates
+ - All edits are peer-reviewed for clarity and alignment
+ - Improvements are tracked and actioned after retrospectives
